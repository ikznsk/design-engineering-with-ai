## 1. CHANI — trust benchmark (research.md/README.md "Еталон якості тексту"; personas.md → Masha, Trust triggers #1)

**What it looks like:** off-white background, blush and lavender used sparingly for hierarchy/state, not decoration. Clear type scale — expressive display type for titles, plain legible body text, small caption-size labels. Collage/star motifs kept restrained and grid-aligned so they frame content instead of competing with it.

**Technique borrowed:** an expressive, slightly personal display typeface for headings (the wow-moment line, section titles) paired with a plain, quiet body face — so the *voice* feels human and specific (per voice.md Principle 2: every claim names a specific chart placement) while the *reading* stays effortless.

**Anxiety it resolves:** personas.md → Masha's #1 trust trigger — "the text reads like a real astrologer, not a template." A generic SaaS type scale reads templated; CHANI's restrained expressiveness reads hand-considered.

Source: [CHANI App](https://www.chani.com/app), [UI/UX Case Study: CHANI](https://medium.com/@info_45537/tl-dr-14868841ed2c)

## 2. Glow (wellness app genre) — Iryna's pick, also the product's own niche name

**What it looks like:** soft pink / clean white / pale green palettes signaling "new beginnings," or soft lilac-to-lavender gradients; Inter-based type with softened weight and generous white space; light, calm, non-clinical.

**Technique borrowed:** the *palette temperature* — soft, warm-leaning neutrals with one gentle accent hue, not a cold SaaS blue. Generous white space between sections (matches wireframes' existing zone-based rhythm).

**Anxiety it resolves:** jtbd.md → Emotional job, "дозвіл бути собою" — a cold, clinical UI would fight the "permission to be yourself" tone; Glow's software is literally used for a bodily/emotional tracking context, so its calm-not-clinical solve is exactly the register Astro Recipe needs for onboarding and results.

Source: [Glow UI](https://www.glowui.com/), [Designing for Calm](https://medium.com/@msjuliabraimova/designing-for-calm-how-i-built-a-soothing-ux-flow-without-a-single-plugin-1ebc033327f6)

## 3. Professional astrologer reports (Etsy / Typewriter Astrology / UpAstrology-style PDFs) — Iryna's pick

**What it looks like:** a real astrologer's paid report reads like a personal document, not an app screen — named sections per planet/house (Sun profile, Moon profile, Rising profile), a chart wheel diagram, editorial pacing, often a slightly warm/paper background instead of pure white.

**Technique borrowed:** treat `report.html` as a *document*, not a fifth app screen — section-per-planet structure (already matches report.html's existing 4 sections), a warm paper-like ground instead of stark white, more generous line-height for long-form reading.

**Anxiety it resolves:** CLAUDE.md → Monetization: this is the thing being paid for — it has to *look* like a $50 report, not a free app screen, or the paywall doesn't feel justified. Also matches CLAUDE.md → Real Use Cases: people already pay Telegram astrologers for exactly this artifact.

Source: [Typewriter Astrology — Birth Chart Report](https://typewriterastrology.com/store/p/birth-chart-report-includes-11-customized-pdfs), [UpAstrology natal report](https://upastrology.com/natal-report)

## 4. Aurora / Ethereal Glow (design trend, not a single product) — found via WebSearch at Iryna's request

**What it looks like:** iridescent gradients, soft blur, translucent overlays, soft pastels with warm blush tones and gentle iridescent hues that shift; rounded or slightly extended letterforms that echo the fluidity of the visual environment. Documented as suited to "wellness apps, tech product launches, or spiritual themes."

**Technique borrowed:** a dark-to-gradient ambient background (not stark white or cream) for the wow-moment / results screen specifically, with a soft glow behind the chart visualization — literal enough to justify the product's own name, "Glow."

**Anxiety it resolves:** jtbd.md → Emotional job ("дозвіл бути собою") plus the wow-moment's job (Emotional job + R1) — a flat white screen undersells the "wow" a chart reading is supposed to deliver; ambient glow signals "something a little magical is happening here" without tipping into CHANI's illustration-heavy whimsy.

Source: [Diffused Glow — Creative Market](https://creativemarket.com/blog/diffused-glow-the-rise-of-soft-atmospheric-design), [Ethereal Design — WebSheen](https://websheen.com/exploring-ethereal-design-in-modern-web-aesthetics/)
