# Influencer & Course Creator Research — Astro Recipe

## Key Insight

Astrology influencers monetize primarily through **education funnels** (courses, memberships, cohorts), not apps or tools. The dominant model is: free content on Instagram/YouTube → paid course or membership ($100–$1,000+ range). CIS influencers focus on "самопізнання" and wealth/manifestation angles. International influencers lean into self-discovery, spirituality, and personal empowerment. No one is selling a self-serve, deeply personalized natal chart product at the intersection of astrology + actionable life guidance — which is Astro Recipe's exact gap.

## Product Archetypes Found

1. **Membership / Inner Circle** — Recurring access to community + curated content (Astrology Hub Inner Circle, $74/mo). Works at scale with large email/social lists. Requires constant content production.

2. **Self-paced Course** — One-time or cohort-based course on natal astrology topics ($67–$297). Most common monetization for influencer astrologers. Low friction for buyer, high margin.

3. **Personalized Report (PDF/Digital)** — Date → automated or semi-custom PDF delivered digitally. CIS model (Matrix Destiny $7–$149); international model (Astrology Hub $27–$37 per report). Low ticket, high volume.

4. **App Subscription** — Monthly access to natal chart + transit readings (CHANI ~$14.99/mo, Co-Star). High development cost, strong retention potential.

5. **"School" / Curriculum Brand** — Full school identity with multiple courses and certifications (Школа 11 Дом, School of Awakening). Positions as education, not just content.

6. **Wealth / Manifestation Angle** — Astrology reframed as practical tool for money/career decisions (Mineeva "Astrological Wealth Strategist"). Very strong in CIS market and growing in EN market.

## Implications for Astro Recipe

- **The gap is real:** No one offers a deeply personalized, self-serve natal chart analysis that reads planets + aspects + houses in combination *and* gives actionable instructions. The market has vague apps (Co-Star, CHANI) OR deep but inaccessible text (Astro.com reports) OR courses that teach you to read charts yourself. Astro Recipe would be the first to give a non-astrologer a "you specifically, here's what to do" experience.

- **CIS market likes wealth/money framing:** Mineeva's "Astrological Wealth Strategist" positioning is the strongest signal. Career, money, and life purpose themes convert well. Style/aesthetic angle is less proven.

- **Report model is validated in CIS:** Matrix Destiny ($7–$149 funnel) and Astrology Hub ($27–$37 per report) both show people will pay for automated, personalized outputs. Price sensitivity in CIS market suggests starting lower ($7–$15 range for an entry product).

- **Education funnel is not our model (yet):** Influencers run courses because they have audiences. Astro Recipe is a product, not a personal brand. The product itself must deliver the value the course promises to teach.

- **"Written by astrologers, not AI"** is a trust signal CHANI leans on. Astro Recipe should think about how to frame algorithmic interpretation credibly — algorithm + expert methodology, not AI hallucination.

- **Katarina Dimova (product owner's suggestion)** was not found as a significant influencer. If the product owner knows a specific person by this name, they should provide a direct link or platform handle.
